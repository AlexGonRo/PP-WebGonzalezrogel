About Me

  My name is Alejandro González Rogel, a software engineer with experience in the field of Big Data, and a profound interest in Artificial Intelligence. Currently living in Spain, I joined the workforce in 2020.

  AI caught my eye when, during my Erasmus, I took a class in bio-inspired algorithms. I think what got me hooked was how AI could tackle complex problems without necessarily having to code the solution. Since then, I have been striving to learn more about the world of machine learning and its real-world applications.

  Over the years, I have participated in both academia and the private sector, experienced the good and bad of living in three different countries, and taken on many diverse professional endeavors. I enjoy the thrill of discovering the unknown, so let us hope I keep facing new challenges in the future!
